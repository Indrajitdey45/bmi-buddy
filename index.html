Why Track BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple yet effective way
to understand whether your weight is in a healthy range.
It helps you stay aware of your fitness level and detect
potential health risks early.

Smart Monitoring:Track small changes in your body over
time and stay consistent with your fitness goals.

Personalized Insights: Get a better understanding of
your health status based on your BMI results.

Early Awareness: Identify possible weight-related risks
and take action before they become serious.

BMI Weight Guide

understand where you stand and what your results mean for your overall health profile.

UNDERWEIGHT

< 18.6

Your body weight is below the recommended range. Focus on nutrient-rich foods and consider strength-building routines to improve overall health.

NORMAL WEIGHT

18.6 - 24.9

You’re in a healthy range. Maintain your lifestyle with balanced nutrition and regular physical activity to stay on track.

OVERWEIGHT

25+

Your weight is slightly above the ideal range. Small, consistent changes in diet and exercise can lead to meaningful improvements.
